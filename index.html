FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about MaxLananas

Who is MaxLananas?

MaxLananas is a 16-year-old developer from Guadeloupe who specializes in kernel development (C & x86 Assembly), Minecraft 1:1 scale building, civic tech, and open-source software. He is a Builder Council member at BuildTheEarth France and has worked with 40+ builders on large-scale Earth reconstruction projects.

What programming languages does MaxLananas use?

MaxLananas works with a wide range of languages including C, x86 Assembly (NASM), C++, C#, Rust, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, and Dart. His primary focus is low-level systems programming and full-stack web development.

What is MaxOS?

MaxOS is an operating system kernel built from scratch by MaxLananas. It features an MBR bootloader, VGA and keyboard drivers, and an interactive terminal — all written in pure C and x86 Assembly. The project is open-source on GitHub.

What Minecraft builds has MaxLananas created?

MaxLananas has created several Minecraft builds including a 1:1 scale Fort Napoléon (Guadeloupe), Haussmannian Parisian buildings, brutalist architecture, and medieval constructions. He has also collaborated on the Bugatti circuit (Le Mans) and Larressingle medieval city with BuildTheEarth France.

What is BuildTheEarth France?

BuildTheEarth (BTE) France is a community project that aims to recreate the entire Earth in Minecraft at 1:1 scale. MaxLananas is a Builder Council member and active contributor, having worked with 40+ builders on projects like the Bugatti circuit and the medieval city of Larressingle.

How can I commission MaxLananas?

You can commission MaxLananas for dev work (web apps, Java plugins, tools) or Minecraft builds through his Discord server. Join the Discord and open a ticket in the commissions channel. Tariffs are transparent and project tracking is provided.
