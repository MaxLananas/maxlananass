Frequently asked questions

How much does a Minecraft build commission cost?

Pricing depends on scale, complexity and timeline. Send a brief on Discord and you'll receive a transparent, free quote within 24 to 48 hours.

How long does a build take to complete?

Turnaround varies from a few days for small structures to several weeks for monumental or 1:1 scale projects. A realistic timeline is confirmed during the quote stage.

What is a 1:1 scale Minecraft build?

A 1:1 scale build is a faithful recreation of a real-world location, translated block by block at true scale using tools like WorldEdit and real-world elevation data.

Can I commission a build for my server or event?

Yes. Spawn areas, event lobbies, competition maps and full server worlds are all part of the regular commission work.

How do I get started with a commission?

Open a ticket on Discord, describe your project, and you'll receive a reply with next steps within 24 to 48 hours.
